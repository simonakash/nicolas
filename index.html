An open house

Does your home meet the criteria to attract many potential buyers? Then an open house is the appropriate approach. Together we organize a professional open house. I will be present that day and provide you with guidance. Moreover, I’ll advise potential buyers, point out the possibilities and answer all possible architectural questions. This way buyers will see the full potential of your home while I’ll look after your interests, and I’ll ensure a smooth sale of your home.

The cost? I work with a combination of a fixed expense allowance and a1% commission on the sales price. A golden deal for both buyer and seller. When the house is not sold on the open house. then you only pay the fixed expense allowance.
